practical no:5
Aim: Automate enteringtext into input fields and clicking buttons on a web page.



Steps:

1.Open the Firefox browser and launch the Selenium IDE extension. 

2.Select "Create a new project" and enter your chosen project name. 

3.Rename the current test case to "Test". 

4.Enter http://omayo.blogspot.com/ into the Playback Base URL field. 

5.Click the "Run Current Test" (play) button to execute the initial script. 

6.Press the "Record" button to start capturing your browser actions. 

7.Navigate to the login section and fill in the username and password. 

8.Click the "Stop Recording" button in the IDE to finish. 

9.Click on the username and password steps to verify they located the login section.
